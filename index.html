About

Andre Hamm is a Costa Rican Slopestyle skier and 2018 Winter Olympic Hopeful. He grew up in Boulder, Colorado with an American father and a Costa Rican mother. He spent his childhood exploring the surrounding Rocky Mountains and makin yearly trips to Costa Rica to play in the ocean and visit his family. Andre started competing in slopestyle skiing when he was 14, after many years of skiing around with his friends. He quickly fell in love with the sport and is now competing at World Cups in hopes of qualifying for the Olympics. He is currently recovering from a concussion and getting ready for the final olympic push!
